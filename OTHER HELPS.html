Delay in return

Hello User, sorry for the inconvenience that you have faced in our returning process. You must have got a message on which the details of the pickup guy's (phone number & name) and the details of the (date & day) on which your pickup is scheduled will have been written. If somehow the (date & day) on which your pickup is scheduled got missed and the pickup didn't happen then you don't need to get worry the pickup guy will reach you by the next day of the missed day. If still the sequel is same, then you can request for return again. For any further enquiry you can make a call on A9 STORE help line number.

    Product Refund

    Product Refund or simply the return of your money that you have paid for purchasing the particular design from the platform. There are three options the platform has provided to the user of which user can choose one among them at a time and these three options are listed below with their details.

  • Replace it with the same product
    The platform will exchange the existing design with a new one, there will be no any process of the product refund in the option.
  • Return the amount on your account
    The amount of the returned or canceled design will come to the bank account that you have linked with the platform. In case you want your amount in another bank account that's available too but, you have to link that account with the platform too.
  • Return the amount on your A9 STORE balance
    All the things will happen the same as written in the back option but, this time the amount will come to your A9 STORE balance.

    • For any further enquiry you can make a call on A9 STORE help line number.
